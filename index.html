About Us

Chanan Procurement and Delivery Services is a purchasing and procurement center whose main objective is to provide world class procurement services to its clients, to the end that our happy clients can fixate their efforts, energy and resources on the strategic differentiators of their respective businesses. At Chanan PDS, we believe that our clients deserve procurement services that are beyond the world class paradigm, which do not only free up valuable time for investment into more core activities, but also help drive low cost significantly without compromising on quality. We provide a perfect mix of latest industry technology and human expertise to provide greater leverage for our clients in the critical business of purchasing and procurement. Our superior network of suppliers made up of thousands of vendors and service providers gives us a close knit relationship with the supply market which enables us to bring to our clients advantages which they cannot find any other way. The company is proudly Ghanaian, and was incorporated in 2015, seeking to use strategic approach and best practices to graduate procurement in Ghana from the traditional methods to a global benchmark. Our team is aptly qualified to handle your procurement more profitably and effectively. We are committed to understanding your business needs, in order to not just bring you a supplier, but a strategic supply chain partner who will not just sell you a product or service, but will offer a perfect solution for your business.

Services

sourcing

Our team of experts is committed to understanding your business needs, in order to bring to you suppliers that will not just sell or provide a product or service, but will render a solution that perfectly meets the need, forming a strategic supply chain partnership in the process. Our pool of peak performing suppliers who span across industries and sectors makes us the go-to company for all your sourcing needs, both locally and globally.

Project-Based Procurement

Whether you’ve won a contract to construct an edifice, organize an event (corporate or social), etc., or it’s your own in-house project, there is expert solution for you. Our commitment is to ensure minimum possible project cost at the given deliverables. Allow expert hands to effectively manage the procurement of goods and services for your project, while you focus on and do what do you best.

Procurement Planning

We provide our clients expert advice and guidance on procurement procedures and best practices in the design and implementation of their activities. We provide advisory role to clients in the formulation of their procurement policies. See us as the procurement department you never had, working with you to streamline your procurement activities, to help you achieve higher value from suppliers while driving low cost.

Contract management

The objective here is to maximize financial and operational performance and minimize risk for our clients. We provide guidance to our clients in best practices of ‘best value selection’, a key upstream or pre-award procedure. We bring to bear our adeptness in negotiation and contract life cycle management, ensuring continued supplier cooperation and compliance throughout the life cycle of the contract.

Delivery Services

Procured goods and services must get to the buyer in time and intact, else procurement has not been successful. We offer a cheaper but not in any way inferior alternative to our clients to ensure procured goods are transported to site in a timely manner.
